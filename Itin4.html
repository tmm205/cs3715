While of course known for its many historical places of interest, Cairo has much more to offer. Ranked by social networking site Badoo as the "world's most 24-hour city", Cairo offers many exciting nightlife possibilities, as well as a number of stunning mosques and gardens for the more relaxation-focussed tourist.

Per Traveladvisor.com, Cairo's best attractions are:

  1. Keops Pyramid
  2. Gizeh Plateau
  3. Sightseeing Tours
  4. Egyptian Antiquities Museum
  5. Saqqara Pyramids
